Is this therapy?

No. AeonAnima doesn't heal you. It shows you what's already there — clearly, without softening it. If you need a therapist, see a therapist. AeonAnima is the thing you use to understand what you'd even say to one.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT knows everything about the world. AeonAnima knows one thing — you. It can't answer from your answers if it doesn't have them. The more you share, the more precise it gets. ChatGPT starts fresh every time. AeonAnima never does.

Is my data private?

Your answers never train other models. They belong to your portrait only. We use end-to-end encryption and your reflections are used solely to power your personal AeonAnima — nothing else.
